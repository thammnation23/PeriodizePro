Match Difficulty Index
Your team's ELO + opponent ratings power the Match Difficulty Index (MDI). Auto-seeds from MLS history; updates from match results.
📖 What do these values mean?
ELO RATING SCALE
1850+Elite — dynasty-level (rare)
1700–1850Top tier — title contenders, MLS Cup level
1550–1700Above average — playoff teams
1450–1550Average — mid-table
1300–1450Bottom — relegation-zone equivalents
<1300Very weak — expansion / lower tier
MATCH DIFFICULTY INDEX (MDI)
0–3EASY — heavy favourite, can train hard the week before
3–5MODERATE — slight favourite
5–6.5CHALLENGING — coin-flip / slight underdog
6.5–8HARD — underdog, taper training, prioritise recovery
8–10VERY HARD — heavy underdog
ELO MOVEMENT AFTER A MATCH
±5 or lessResult was expected
±10–15Moderately surprising
±20Big upset (max with K=20)
MDI formula: based on Robertson & Joyce (2014) match difficulty index from logistic regression, adapted to use ELO ratings instead of league rank. Includes 100-pt home advantage adjustment per FIFA-style football ELO. Updated via standard ELO formula (K=20).
